About ABQ Desi Bites

Bringing the heart of South India to the Land of Enchantment

Every Bite, A Memory

ABQ Desi Bites was born from a simple passion — the love of authentic South Indian and Hyderabadi home cooking. Founded in 2025, we set out to bring the bold, rich flavors of Hyderabad, Chennai, and beyond to the people of Albuquerque, New Mexico.

Every dish we serve is made from scratch, using traditional recipes and fresh ingredients. Whether it's the crispy golden dosa, the aromatic Hyderabadi Biryani, or the warm Gulab Jamun — each bite is crafted to transport you straight to the streets of South India.

We believe good food creates good memories. That's our promise with every plate we serve.
